## Event Details

Join us for our annual Volunteer Appreciation event! This special cleanup is our way of saying thank you to all our amazing volunteers who make our work possible. We'll clean along the Potomac River shoreline at Widewater State Park, followed by a thank-you picnic with food, drinks, and prizes.

**All volunteers welcome - whether you've been with us for years or this is your first event!**

New volunteers are especially encouraged to join and meet our community!

---
## Location

Widewater State Park<br/>
1571 Widewater Beach Rd, Stafford, VA 22554

## What We Provide

- Trash grabbers
- Gloves
- Reflective vests
- Trash bags
- First Aid Kit
- Picnic lunch (burgers, hot dogs, veggie options)
- Cold drinks
- Raffle prizes

Bring water for during the cleanup, wear sturdy shoes, and dress for warm weather. All ages welcome—kids under 18 must be accompanied by an adult.
