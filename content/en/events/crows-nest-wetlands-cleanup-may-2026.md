## Event Details

Join us for a community cleanup day at Crow's Nest along Accokeek Creek! This important wetland ecosystem provides critical habitat for wildlife and helps filter water before it reaches the Potomac River. Let's work together to protect this valuable natural resource. This is an on the water cleanup and you will need to bring a kayak and PFD. Those who wish to attend but don't have a kayak can clean up the parking lot and hiking trails.
